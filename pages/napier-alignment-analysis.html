Improvement Opportunity

Develop a comprehensive strategy to strengthen Te Tiriti o Waitangi integration across all aspects of school operations, with specific focus on culturally responsive pedagogy and partnerships with mana whenua.

Key Recommendations for Action Plan Improvement

  1. Strengthen Te Ao Māori integration - Develop a comprehensive plan to integrate Te Ao Māori perspectives across all curriculum areas and school practices in the next iteration of the Action Plan.
  2. Enhance culturally responsive pedagogy - Include more specific professional development initiatives in the Action Plan to strengthen teachers' culturally responsive teaching practices.
  3. Deepen partnerships with mana whenua - Outline formal partnership agreements with local iwi in the Action Plan to support authentic engagement and collaboration.
  4. Strengthen evaluation practices - Include more robust systems in the Action Plan for evaluating the impact of improvement initiatives on student outcomes.
  5. Enhance student agency - Add specific strategies to the Action Plan for increasing student voice and leadership in curriculum design and school improvement processes.
